Buyer Help

Browse products on the homepage, click on a product you like, then click Buy Now or Add to Cart. Follow the checkout steps to enter your delivery address and complete payment.
RadiusX uses your GPS location or pincode to show you products and shops available near you. Allow location access when prompted for the best experience. You can also set your location manually by clicking the location box in the navbar.
Go to Orders from the top navigation. You'll see all your orders with their current status. Click on any order for detailed tracking information.
Yes. Orders can be cancelled within 1 hour of placing them. For returns, raise a request within 7 days of delivery. Go to Orders → select order → Request Return. The seller will review and process it within 48 hours.
RadiusX accepts UPI, debit/credit cards, net banking, and Cash on Delivery (COD) for eligible orders. Payment options may vary by seller.

Seller Help

Click Sell on RadiusX on the login page. Fill in your business details, store information, and upload the required documents (PAN card, business proof, bank details). Our team reviews applications within 24-48 hours.
You will need:

• PAN card (mandatory)
• GST certificate (if applicable)
• Business proof (shop registration / trade licence)
• Cancelled cheque or bank passbook

All documents must be clear, valid, and in JPG, PNG, or PDF format.
Once approved, log in and go to your Seller Dashboard. Click Add Product, fill in the product details including name, description, price, stock quantity, and images. Products go live immediately after being saved.
Payments are settled to your registered bank account within 7 business days after order delivery confirmation. A platform commission of 5-10% (depending on category) is deducted before settlement.
Check the rejection email for the specific reason. Common reasons include blurry documents, mismatched PAN details, or invalid GST numbers. Fix the issue and apply again from the login page using the same email address.

Account & Security

On the login page, click Forgot Password? Enter your registered email address and we'll send you a reset link. The link expires in 30 minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.
Click the location box in the top navigation bar. You can add a new address manually or detect your current location via GPS. Saved addresses are stored in your profile and can be managed from the Profile page.
Yes. RadiusX uses industry-standard encryption (bcrypt for passwords, JWT for sessions). We never sell your personal data. Your location is only used to show relevant local products and is never shared with third parties without your consent.
Go to your Profile page → Account Settings → Delete Account. This action is irreversible. All your data will be permanently deleted within 30 days. Active orders must be resolved before deletion.
